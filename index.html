About Me

I am a 3rd year, undergraduate student at the University of California, Merced. I major in Computer Science and Engineereing and in the Fall 2022, I hope to start my Masters in Human-Computer Interaction and/or UX/UI development.

In my free time, I love to paint. In a world as complicated as this one, painting helps relive stress and allows me to forget about my worries for a little while.

While I do want to work as a UX/UI developer, I also want to use that knowledge to help people in underserved communities. I want people to be able to get access to a laptop or a phone whenever they need without worrying about whether they can buy food for the month. I want to use my skills to make it easier for people to find help online and make the world a little less complicated for them.
